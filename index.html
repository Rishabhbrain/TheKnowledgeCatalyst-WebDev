Welcome to The Knowledge Catalyst , your weekly source of in-depth analysis and thought-provoking discussions. I am Rishabh Modi, a curious and dedicated researcher, journalist, and podcaster. With a passion for exploring the most interesting and relevant topics of our time, I bring you a unique perspective and a fresh take on the issues that matter most. Each week, I delve into a new theme, inviting expert guests, analysts, and everyday people to share their insights and experiences. From science and technology to politics and culture, The Knowledge Catalyst offers a platform for in-depth examination and meaningful conversation. My goal is to challenge the status quo, spur critical thinking, and foster a sense of community among listeners. Whether you're a lifelong learner, a skeptical skeptic, or just looking for a new way of looking at the world, The Knowledge catalyst has something for you. Join me on this journey of discovery and exploration as we delve into the most fascinating and thought-provoking topics of our time.
